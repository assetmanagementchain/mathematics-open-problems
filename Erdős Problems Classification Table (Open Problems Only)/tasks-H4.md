# Erdős Problems — H4 (Open, Structurally Unresolved)

> This page lists **genuine H4 Erdős problems**:
>
> Problems that are **still open**, **not downgraded to H3**, and
> whose difficulty arises from **missing structural frameworks** rather than
> proof complexity.
>
> These problems typically involve:
> - primes-driven arithmetic
> - geometric / continuous limits
> - covering systems and self-referential constraints
> - analytic–combinatorial mismatches
>
> They are **not short-term attack targets**, but serve as
> benchmarks for new mathematical structures.

## Notes

- Graph-theoretic Ramsey / Turán problems are **not listed here**
  (tracked as H3 unless structurally downgraded).
- Unit fraction, factorial, binomial-coefficient problems are
  mostly H2/H3 and intentionally excluded.
- This list represents **true structural H4 boundaries** in the Erdős corpus.
